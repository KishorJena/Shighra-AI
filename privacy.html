Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2025

Hey, Privacy Fans!

I’m Kishor Jena, the guy behind Shighr.ai—your speedy AI chat buddy for Chrome. I’m all about keeping things simple and respecting your privacy. Here’s the lowdown on how we roll with your data (or don’t!).

Data? What Data?

Shighr.ai is chill about your info. Here’s what we do (and don’t do):

  • No Cloud Shenanigans: We don’t send your stuff to servers or the cloud. It all stays cozy on your device.
  • Local Vibes Only: Your prompts and settings? Stored right in Chrome’s chrome.storage.local. Fast, private, and 100% yours.
  • Your Groq Key (Or Ours, At First): You can grab your own API key from Groq and pop it in via our input field—we’ll use that to chat with their AI. Initially, we provide our own Groq API key to get you started, but if our limit hits or we turn it off, you’ll need your own. Either way, we don’t store your key or peek at it.

Bottom line: We don’t collect personal or sensitive stuff like your name, email, browsing history, or secret cookie stash. Nada!

How Shighr.ai Works Its Magic

We overlay our AI assistant on your tab using a Shadow DOM—fancy tech that keeps us separate from the page you’re on. No meddling, just helping! When you send a prompt, we pair it with a system prompt to keep replies short and simple, staying true to our quick-chat motto.

Third-Party Pals

We team up with Groq to power the AI. Your prompt (plus our system prompt for snappy replies) goes to them via the API key (ours at first, or yours later). That’s it—no other buddies in the mix. Curious about their side? Check Groq’s Privacy Policy.

Future Stuff

This is how we roll now. If we ever tweak things—like adding server storage later—we’ll update this policy and give you a heads-up. No sneaky moves here!

Got Questions?

No fine print nonsense—I’m an open book! Hit me up on GitHub, LinkedIn, or X if you wanna chat privacy or just say hi.
